Advantages of Blood Donation

  • Saves Lives – One donation can save up to three lives.
  • Promotes Heart Health – Reduces excess iron levels, lowering heart disease risk.
  • Helps in Weight Regulation – Burns approximately 650 kcal per donation.
  • Reduces Cancer Risk – Lower iron levels may decrease cancer risks.
  • Encourages a Healthy Lifestyle – Encourages regular health monitoring and good habits.
